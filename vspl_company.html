About Us

Vizag Seaport Private Limited ( VSPL ) is a Modern Bulk Cargo Handing Terminal operating EQ-8 and EQ-9 Berts in the inner Harbour of Visakhapatnam Port on the East Coast of India. VSPL has been awarded a 30 years License Period to operate these two Berths on BOT ( Build, Operate and Transfer ) basis, in the year 2001, under the provisions of Major Port Trust Act 1963. VSPL has a Cargo handling capacity of 9.5 Million Metric Tons per annum.

Visakhapatnam Port Trust (“VPT”) has on completion of dredging to -16.10 Meters to berth fully laden Panamax vessels, in the year 2016, has declared EQ-8 & EQ-9 berths of VSPL as eligible berths to handle fully laden Panamax Vessels arriving at a draught of - 14.5 Meters. It is pertinent to note that at present VSPL is the only multipurpose terminal at Vizag Port to handle fully laden panamax vessels. With this long pending infrastructure facility of deep draught having been achieved, VSPL is now poised to achieve its full potential.

Vizag Seaport provides efficient cargo handling services for a variety of bluk and break bluk cargo groups including Coal, Iron Ore, Limestone, During the year 2017-18, VSPL has handled a cargo volume of 7.1 Million Metric Tonnes and cargo volume of 7.5 Million Metric Tons is projected to achieve in the current financial year 2018-19.

VSPL TERMINAL :

Deep drafted Bulk Terminal licensed to mechanically handle coal/coke/iron ore/fines/Gypsum, Fertilizers/Fertilizers Raw Materials and all other Bulk and Break Bulk Gargoes including Project Cargoes. The two Multipurpose Berths are equipped with capacity to handle 9.5 Million Tons Per Annum ( MTPA). The terminal has wide range of New Generation Port Handling Equipment for efficient handling of cargoes with less turnaround time of vessels to economize the ocean logistics for shippers and receivers.
